The End of the Gaokao, A New Journey Awaits

发表于2023-06-10|更新于2025-08-07|英文期刊
|总字数:497|阅读时长:3分钟|浏览量:

Gaokao Farewell

In the 2023 Gaokao, the author once again served as a volunteer for the farewell service, working in Haidian District, Beijing. You made your appearance in the exam halls at your prime, and we sustained your dreams at our posts.

The Gaokao offers you a very important opportunity to change your life and the fate of your family, creating a better environment for your elderly parents to live in. Therefore, for children from poor families, the Gaokao is a very important choice for them, as this path will change everything in their lives.

Gaokao Conclusion

At 5:00 PM on June 10th, the final bell rang for the geography exam, marking the end of the Gaokao. Finally, the Gaokao is over! This day is a moment worth celebrating for students, parents, and teachers alike. The long period of tense preparation and anxious waiting has now become the past. It’s time to let go of the burdens and embrace the joyful new journey ahead!

The Gaokao is like a challenging journey, where we have written our youth with sweat and effort. Over the years, we have put in countless hard hours, tirelessly studying books and papers. Now, at last, we have reached a new starting point, a brand new chapter in our lives.

The conclusion of the Gaokao brings not just relief and ease, but also a deep sense of joy. That feeling of happiness and release from the heart is indescribable. We can let go of the pressure and anxiety, and enjoy our own free time. Stepping out of the exam hall, the bright sky seems to have been prepared just for us, making us feel joyful and exhilarated.

The end of the Gaokao means we will bid farewell to the mountains of books and endless exercises, and to the days of studying late into the night. Now is the time to cross one threshold after another and embark on our own paths to dreams. Whether continuing our education or pursuing our interests, we will have more choices and opportunities.

A new journey is about to begin. Let us step forward bravely, carrying our hopes for the future. Graduation season is approaching, filled with farewells and reunions, but more importantly, it is an opportunity for growth and transformation. We will leave our familiar campus and integrate into a broader community. We will meet new friends, face new challenges, and pursue our own life values.

The conclusion of the Gaokao brings a sense of joy, but more importantly, it has shaped us. We are no longer just Gaokao candidates, but individuals with dreams, aspirations, and the ability to create a bright future. Let us carry this joy and confidence as we embark on our new journey, opening our own wonderful chapters!

The conclusion of the Gaokao, a new journey is about to begin. Let’s cheer for ourselves at this special moment! Wishing all Gaokao students a bright future and even greater success! Keep going!
