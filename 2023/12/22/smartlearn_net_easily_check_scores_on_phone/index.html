Zhixue.com: Easily Check Scores on Your Mobile Device

发表于2023-12-22|更新于2025-08-07|英文期刊
|总字数:834|阅读时长:5分钟|浏览量:

Introduction

Due to policy restrictions, Zhixue.com is no longer allowed to publicly display students’ exam scores, which has caused significant inconvenience. We are unable to promptly know our scores or rankings, which is detrimental to our studies.

The Quitting School Website can bypass the restrictions of Zhixue.com to access your actual exam scores. It is an open-source project developed using Python3 and can only run on devices that support the Python environment.

In this article, I will introduce two Zhixue.com score-checking apps that can run on mobile devices.

Zhixue.com Lite Version

The software is very simple and clean, with no ads, only retaining the functions of exams and assignments. The software size is significantly reduced, and the running speed is greatly improved.

After successful login, you can see all the exam names:

Compared to the multi-level menu design of the official version, this version has a simple and clear interface with well-defined functional categories. The software provides a user-friendly interaction method and intuitive visual effects, making it easy for users to master the operation. The user interface layout is reasonable, with prominent icons and buttons for easy identification. The operations follow simple and intuitive principles, reducing the learning curve for users. Whether swiping the screen, clicking buttons, or long-pressing, users can quickly get started. The simplicity and ease of use are key features of this software, allowing users to quickly obtain the information they need and reduce operational steps and time costs. Through simple taps and swipes, users can easily complete operations such as score inquiries and assignment reviews. This not only enhances the user experience but also makes the software a preferred alternative to the official version.

Clicking on an exam name will display the report details, including scores for each subject and the total score:

The report details feature uses a pop-up module design, allowing users to view the report details without leaving the current page. This design effectively enhances the user experience. Swiping up reveals the scores for each subject, while swiping down quickly closes the pop-up. The scores for each subject are displayed using progress bars, making it easy for users to see their performance. The program also automatically calculates the total score ratio based on the overall score, giving users a clearer understanding of their performance.

More functional pages:

Clicking on the score entries for different subjects will take you to more functional pages, providing a more detailed understanding of that subject.

  1. Question Analysis: The question analysis feature provides detailed solutions and explanations for each question. Whether it’s mathematical formulas, historical events, or complex scientific experiments, users can find detailed explanations and steps within the app. This not only helps users understand the questions but also expands their knowledge and deepens their understanding of the concepts.
  2. Study Buddy: The study buddy feature allows users to invite friends to study together. The two can jointly create study plans, share study insights and materials, supervise and encourage each other, and make progress together. This not only adds fun to learning but also fosters teamwork and mutual support.
  3. Class/Grade Ranking: The class/grade ranking feature allows users to easily check their performance rankings in class or grade. This helps users understand their learning status within the group, identify their strengths and weaknesses, and motivate themselves to continuously improve.
  4. Original Paper Review: The original paper review feature allows users to revisit past exam papers. This is very helpful for review and consolidation of knowledge. Users can clearly see their previous mistakes and make targeted improvements.
  5. Original Paper Download: The original paper download feature allows users to download past exam papers to their local devices. This way, users can review and study the papers even without an internet connection. Additionally, the downloaded papers can be saved as digital materials for easy access.

Through these features, the aim is to provide users with a comprehensive and efficient learning platform to help them better master knowledge and improve their scores.
